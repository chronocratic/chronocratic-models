<objective>
Collapse BasicEncodingMixin from a 4-hook contract to a 2-hook contract (_get_encoder -> nn.Module, _encode_batch) and add gradient_enabled: bool = False to encode() on both mixin families.

Purpose: The 4-hook contract (_get_encoder + _get_encoder_module + _prepare_inputs + _postprocess) is broken by design — TST and Series2Vec returned bound methods from _get_encoder(), defeating the auto-detect in _get_encoder_module(). The 2-hook contract separates concerns honestly: module for eval/train state toggle, callable for compute. gradient_enabled enables adversarial attacks and contrastive-view comparison that need gradients through the encoder.

Output: Rewritten BasicEncodingMixin, updated dilated mixin, 7 model refactors, migrated tests.
</objective>
